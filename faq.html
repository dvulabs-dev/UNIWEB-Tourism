Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the tour packages?

Our standard tour packages include accommodation, transportation during the tour, a local English-speaking guide, and entry fees to main attractions. Flights and personal expenses are generally excluded.

Do I need a visa to travel to Indonesia?

Many nationalities are eligible for Visa On Arrival (VOA) or visa-free entry. Please check the official Indonesian immigration website for the most up-to-date requirements for your specific country.

How can I make a payment?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) and bank transfers. A 20% deposit is required upon booking to secure your reservation.

Is it safe to travel solo?

Yes! Indonesia is generally very safe for solo travelers. Our group tours are a great way for solo travelers to meet like-minded people. We also offer private solo tours.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days or more prior to departure are fully refundable minus a small processing fee. Cancellations made within 30 days are subject to varying penalty fees.
